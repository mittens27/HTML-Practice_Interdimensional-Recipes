Quantum Quiche

Quantum Quiche: A savory pie that can alter your reality with every slice.

Ingredients

  • 1 9-inch pie crust
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups of heavy cream
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon of nutmeg
  • 1 cup of shredded cheese
  • 1/4 cup of quantum particles (available at your nearest interdimensional market)
  • Any fillings of your choice (such as bacon, spinach, mushrooms, etc.)
  1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F and place the pie crust in a pie dish.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk the eggs, cream, salt, pepper, nutmeg, and quantum particles.
  3. Sprinkle the cheese and fillings over the pie crust, then pour the egg mixture over them.
  4. Bake for 35 to 40 minutes or until set.
  5. Let it cool slightly before serving and enjoy your quantum experience!
