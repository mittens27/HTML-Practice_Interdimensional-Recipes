Multiverse Muffins

Multiverse Muffins: A sweet snack that can give you a glimpse of other possible worlds with every bite.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1/4 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 cup of milk
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup of multiverse crystals (available at your nearest interdimensional market)
  1. Preheat oven to 444Â°F and line a 12-cup muffin tin with paper liners.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  3. In another bowl, whisk the oil, egg, milk, vanilla, and multiverse crystals.
  4. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Do not overmix.
  5. Spoon the batter into the prepared muffin tin, filling each cup about 2/3 full.
  6. Bake for 15 to 20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.

Let them cool slightly before serving and enjoy your multiverse exploration!
